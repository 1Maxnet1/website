=== Displays / Cameras


* 1x HDMI
* 2x DSI
* 1x eDP (Instead of HDMI1)
* 1x LVDS (not available when dual-mode DSI)
* 1x CSI 4-line

=== Connectivity

* 1x Ethernet (1Gbit)
* 1x USB 2.0 OTG
* 1x SD Card (SD)
* 1x PCIe 1-Line
* 28x GPIO (TBD)
